Kovarianz (Cov)

$$ Cov(X,Y) = \frac{1}{n} \sum (x_i - \bar{x})(y_i - \bar{y}) $$

Pearson Korrelation (r)

$$ r = \frac{Cov(X,Y)}{\sigma_x \cdot \sigma_y} $$
